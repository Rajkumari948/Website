HTML list Demonstration

In HTML, both <ul> (Unordered list) are <ol> (ordered list) are used to display lists. The main difference is that:

  • <ul> shows items with bullets(., o, etc)
  • <ol> shows items with numbers or letters(1, 2, 3....or A, B, C....)
